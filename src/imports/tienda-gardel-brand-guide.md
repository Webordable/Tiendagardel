Strategic Foundation

• Positioning angle
Tienda Gardel is positioned as the guardian of artisanal craftsmanship in Argentina — not a mass-produced chess product, but a heritage object handcrafted with patience, wood knowledge, and aesthetic integrity. The board is framed as a lifelong companion and an heirloom-worthy piece.

• Target sophistication
Medium to high awareness. The audience understands quality differences, appreciates craftsmanship, and is willing to pay for authenticity.

• Emotional driver
Pride of ownership + cultural identity + intellectual elegance.

• Core differentiator
Single handcrafted chess board model, made in Argentina, rustic-artisanal aesthetic, nationwide delivery, limited production feel.

Visual Direction

• Mood & intensity
Warm, grounded, tactile, intimate. Rustic workshop energy blended with refined minimalism. Emotionally rich but not loud.

• Color logic
Primary palette:
– Deep walnut brown
– Warm oak beige
– Matte charcoal
– Muted cream
– Subtle Argentine-inspired accent (desaturated sky blue used minimally)

Textures:
– Raw wood grain
– Linen fabric overlays
– Soft paper texture backgrounds
– Subtle dust/light particles

• Typography system
Primary Serif: Elegant classic serif with old-world character (e.g., Cormorant / Playfair style)
Secondary Sans: Clean humanist sans-serif for UI clarity
Numbers: Slightly condensed serif for price emphasis
Spanish (Spain) main language typography refinement: refined punctuation, slightly tighter letter spacing
Latin Spanish & English switch adjusts microcopy tone

• Spatial philosophy
Breathing space, generous margins, asymmetrical grid
Text never boxed aggressively
Content flows like a story
Layered depth with foreground wood textures over soft background gradients

• Depth & motion logic
Parallax wood textures
Soft fade-in transitions
Subtle floating light dust particles
Hover effects feel handcrafted, not digital

Full Master Prompt

Design a premium multi-page artisanal e-commerce website for “Tienda Gardel”, a handcrafted chess board brand based in Argentina. The website sells only one product: a handmade wooden chess board. Delivery is available throughout Argentina including Buenos Aires, La Plata, and City Bell.

The primary language is Spanish (Spain). Include a visible language toggle allowing switch between ES (Spain), ES (Latin America), and English. The toggle must be elegant, minimal, and wood-textured, located in the top navigation.

Style direction: rustic artisanal luxury. Warm walnut and oak color palette, soft paper textures, visible wood grain overlays, natural lighting photography, tactile depth, intimate workshop atmosphere. The design must feel handcrafted, emotional, and heritage-driven — not modern tech.

Typography: elegant serif for headlines, refined humanist sans-serif for body and UI. Large expressive headline typography with generous spacing.

Landing page must use split-screen hero layout: left side emotional headline in Spanish, right side high-resolution close-up of the chess board. Subtle parallax motion on scroll.

Include sections:
– Emotional transformation section (industrial vs artisanal contrast)
– Single product authority showcase with price emphasis and nationwide shipping included
– Craftsmanship preview with horizontal scrolling workshop imagery
– Minimal but powerful testimonial section
– National shipping reassurance section
– Immersive final CTA block

Primary CTA throughout: “Comprar ahora”
Secondary CTA: “Descubrir la historia” or “Consultar disponibilidad”

Include scarcity messaging subtly: limited seasonal production.

Create a dedicated craftsmanship storytelling page with immersive photography and process breakdown.

Create a shipping & guarantee page with structured FAQ and delivery clarity for all Argentina.

Include refined micro-interactions: hover warmth shifts, subtle zoom on product, soft fade transitions between language changes.

Avoid generic template layouts. Introduce asymmetry, layered depth, and narrative pacing. Maintain strong visual hierarchy and conversion focus.

The final result must feel like an heirloom brand, emotionally resonant, conversion-driven, and visually premium — built for Figma production-ready design.
